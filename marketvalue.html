Sept 2025 GST reform: The Rs 400/t flat GST Compensation Cess was abolished and replaced with 18% GST (up from 5%). The government's stated uniform tax incidence is now 39.81% of base price = ~28.5% of all-in price. Source: PIB

Methodology & Sources

Coal: Country-level production from IEA Coal 2025, NBS (China), Ministry of Coal India, ESDM (Indonesia), EIA (USA). Prices use country-specific benchmarks. India: CIL blended all-in price ~$31/t; post-Sep 2025 levy burden = 39.81% on base = ~28.5% of total (PIB). Washed coal just 0.3% of CIL output (2.4 Mt) despite 39 MTPA washery capacity.

Oil: Total petroleum & other liquids from EIA STEO: 103 mb/d (2024). Crude + condensate ~82.5 mb/d priced by country-specific benchmarks (WTI $76.55, Urals $70, WCS $61, Brent $80.50). NGLs ~14 mb/d ($185B, Global Market Insights). Biofuels, CTL/GTL, other liquids add ~$143B. Country table shows crude + condensate only; headline includes all liquids at $2,670B.

Gas: 4,124 bcm marketed production (Energy Institute SR 2025, 74th ed). Market value uses IGU WGPS 2025 regional averages. ~31% consumed at <$2/MMBtu (subsidized).

Commodity Comparison: EIA/IEA (fossil fuels), BNEF/IEA WEI 2024 (clean energy), World Steel/Platts (iron ore), ICSG (copper), World Gold Council, USGS/Adamas (rare earths).
