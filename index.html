About This Dashboard

This dashboard brings together disparate and underused datasets on global fossil fuel markets across 10 analysis sections. The sections follow a narrative arc: coal provides a modest share of energy but outsized harm — yet it persists because it serves governments through revenue, jobs, energy security, and political support.

All data is sourced from public datasets (IEA, EIA, Energy Institute, Ember, Global Energy Monitor, OpenSecrets, World Bank, and national statistics). Every data point is traceable to a specific source.
