Comprehensive data on coal, oil & gas markets — value, jobs, subsidies, investment & more

About This Dashboard

This dashboard brings together disparate and underused datasets on global fossil fuel markets across 12 analysis sections. Each section covers a different dimension of the fossil fuel economy — from market value and employment to subsidies, political donations, and phaseout progress.

All data is sourced from public datasets (IEA, EIA, Energy Institute, Ember, Global Energy Monitor, OpenSecrets, World Bank, and national statistics). Every data point is traceable to a specific source.
